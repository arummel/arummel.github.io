Lecture and laboratory
------
I taught 1st year medical students in the team-taught Gross Human Anatomy course. I prepared labs, dissections, example practicals, and scored exams, as well as mentored and guided students in their preparation. I also lectured - giving a functional anatomy lecture on the gastrointestinal tract and a lecture on the upper extremity and brachial plexus.
